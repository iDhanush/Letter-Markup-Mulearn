Cultural Dance Studies

As part of my previous postdoctoral research, I studied traditional cultural dances. To answer your curiosity, my top three favorite dances, along with descriptions, are:

  • Pacific Reef Dance
    Originating from Pacific island communities, this dance mimics the ocean’s waves and coral reef movements to celebrate marine biodiversity.
  • Nordic Aurora Shuffle
    Inspired by the northern lights, this dance features synchronized steps under the open sky, symbolizing unity with nature.
  • Desert Mirage Waltz
    Practiced in arid regions, this slow, flowing dance embodies the shifting sands and mirage illusions.
